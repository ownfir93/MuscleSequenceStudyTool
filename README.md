# Muscle Sequence Study Tool

A single-file, offline-friendly study aid for memorizing **The Sequence of Muscle Contraction** (9 steps), built for iPad/Safari but works in any modern browser.

**Live page:** https://ownfir93.github.io/MuscleSequenceStudyTool/

### Modes
- 📖 **Learn** — all 9 steps with colour-coded key terms; cross-bridge cycle (steps 4–8) flagged
- 🃏 **Flashcards** — hint-based recall with a "More hint" reveal
- 🔢 **Order It** — tap the steps into the correct sequence
- ✍️ **Fill Blanks** — multiple-choice cloze on the key terms
- 🎮 **Visualize** — circular mini-game that animates each step and quizzes you to advance

Wording follows the author's A&P course notes. Progress is saved locally in the browser.
